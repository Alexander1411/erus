# Prompt for Rewriting Privacy Policy and Terms of Use

## Context
This is a Russian pain management clinic website located in Moscow. The clinic provides direct medical consultations with:
- Neurosurgeons (нейрохирург)
- Neurologists (невролог) 
- Rehabilitation specialists (реабилитолог)

The website is used for:
- Providing information about pain management services
- Collecting patient information for appointment scheduling
- Medical secretary contacts patients to schedule appointments and provide additional details if needed

All data is stored on Russian servers, in Russia, following Russian data protection laws and best practices.

## Task
Rewrite both `privacy_policy.html` and `terms.html` to be:
- Simple, clear, non-technical language
- Standard and transparent
- Compliant with Russian law (152-ФЗ)
- Appropriate length (not too short, not overly detailed)
- Written for a medical clinic (not a coordination service)

## Privacy Policy Requirements

Must include (6-10 paragraphs, simple language):

1. **What data is collected:**
   - Name (фамилия, имя, отчество)
   - Contact phone
   - Email address
   - Medical information (pain locations, duration, symptoms) - for appointment preparation
   - Technical data (IP, browser type, visit time)

2. **Why data is collected:**
   - To schedule appointments with doctors
   - For medical secretary to contact patient
   - To prepare doctor for consultation
   - To provide medical services

3. **Data storage location:**
   - Explicitly state: All data stored on servers in Russia
   - Reference to 152-ФЗ requirement

4. **Who has access:**
   - Medical secretary (for scheduling and contact)
   - Doctors (neurosurgeon, neurologist, rehabilitation specialist) - for consultation preparation
   - Only staff directly involved in patient care

5. **User rights:**
   - Right to withdraw consent at any time
   - How to withdraw (email: info@erusmedical.ru)
   - Right to access, correct, or delete data

6. **Reference to 152-ФЗ:**
   - Mention Federal Law 152-ФЗ "On Personal Data"
   - Compliance with Russian data protection laws

7. **Contact information:**
   - Email: info@erusmedical.ru
   - Address: 3-я Тверская-Ямская улица 44, Москва

8. **Data retention:**
   - How long data is stored (standard medical records retention period)

## Terms of Use Requirements

Must include (5-8 paragraphs, simple language):

1. **Website purpose:**
   - Information about pain management clinic services
   - Appointment request form
   - NOT for emergency care (explicitly state: call 103 or 112 for emergencies)

2. **Medical services:**
   - Clinic provides consultations with neurosurgeon, neurologist, rehabilitation specialist
   - Medical advice is only provided by doctor during in-person consultation
   - Website information is for informational purposes only

3. **Appointment process:**
   - User submits information through form
   - Medical secretary will contact user to schedule appointment
   - Secretary may ask additional questions if needed

4. **User responsibilities:**
   - Provide accurate and truthful information
   - Not use website for illegal purposes

5. **Limitation of liability:**
   - No responsibility for technical outages
   - No responsibility for typos or errors on website
   - No responsibility for internet connectivity issues
   - Medical services provided by licensed doctors, clinic is responsible for its services

6. **Data processing:**
   - Reference to Privacy Policy
   - Consent to data processing for appointment scheduling

## Style Guidelines

- Use simple, clear Russian language
- Avoid overly technical legal jargon
- Avoid complex legal disclaimers
- Use standard medical clinic language
- Be transparent and straightforward
- Keep paragraphs concise
- Use professional but accessible tone
- Focus on patient understanding

## Important Notes

- This is a REAL medical clinic providing REAL medical services
- NOT a coordination/matching service
- Doctors work directly for/with the clinic
- Medical secretary is clinic staff
- All services are provided by the clinic itself
- Everything is in Russia, on Russian servers, following Russian law

## Output Format

Rewrite both files maintaining the existing HTML structure and CSS styling, but replace all content with the new simplified, appropriate text following the requirements above.
